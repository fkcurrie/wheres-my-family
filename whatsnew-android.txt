Where's my family!! • Version 1.0.22 (Build 22) Release Notes:

- 💾 Offline-First SQLite Transaction Store: Intermittent connectivity is no longer an issue! All coordinates are persistently queued in SQLite when offline and drained automatically upon reconnection.
- ⚡ 90% Network Polyline Compression: Integrated high-density Google Polyline compression with delta-timestamp encoding to shrink coordinate payloads by up to 90%.
- 🔐 Hardware Enclave Security: Transitioned local E2EE privacy keys from AsyncStore into secure, hardware-backed expo-secure-store enclaves.
- 💤 Dynamic Geofencing Wakeups: Drastically reduced battery consumption when stationary by shutting down high-frequency GPS engines and utilizing passive 100m exit geofencing triggers.
